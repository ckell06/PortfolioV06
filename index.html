I love the design process, and applying it to websites and digital products. So, that’s what I do!

I design for web and digital products. I’m enthusiastic about any opportunity to practice my skills and/or to learn new ones. I’m especially interested in projects that dig deep into concepting and ideation.

For digital, I design for user experience, user interface and interactions. I build websites using Readymag for less complex designs or Webflow when I need apply the use of some JavaScript.

I took the long route to becoming a designer, and I learned lots of valuable lessons along the way...

I started off my professional career teaching high-school math; a year in each Kuwait and Dublin, and 4 years in Beijing, China. I have rich international experience from my time living abroad and travelling during the (conveniently long) teacher’s holidays.
